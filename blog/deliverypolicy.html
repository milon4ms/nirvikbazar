Effective Date: April 2025

📦 1. Delivery Timeline

We aim to deliver your orders within the following timeframes:

🔸 Note: Delivery time may vary depending on product availability, delivery location, and courier operations.

💰 2. Delivery Charges

Our delivery charges are fair and transparent:

💵 3. Payment Method (Cash on Delivery)

✔️ Pay only when you receive your product
✔️ No advance payment required

📍 4. Order Tracking

  • After order confirmation, a tracking number will be sent via SMS or Email
